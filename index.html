Applies only to knockout matches. The player earns this on top of their score points when they correctly call how the match is decided — Full Time vs Extra Time on a winning prediction, or who wins the shootout on a draw prediction. Requires getting the winning/advancing side right.

Why one new row (vs the alternatives we considered)

One new row ✓Reuses the exact mental model the merchant already knows (it's just another outcome tier). One field, configurable, no new concepts. Recommended.
Toggle + valueAn on/off switch that reveals the field. Marginally tidier, but hides the mechanic and adds a state to reason about for no real gain.
Automatic / fixedBake in a fixed bonus, zero setup. Lowest friction but the merchant can't tune it or zero it out — and they lose visibility that knockouts score differently.
Per-stage configSeparate reward maps for group vs knockout. Most control, but it's a real complexity jump and contradicts today's "one mapping for the whole game" model.
