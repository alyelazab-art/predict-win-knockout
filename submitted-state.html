How to read this: both options reuse the real phone shell, header, stats and card — only the body changes after submit, and the decider is finally visible (the fix).

The decider bonus is the merchant’s setting (toggle up top). With a bonus on, the “+N” and the optional Full/Extra-time call appear, and Option A nudges players who skip it. With no bonus, that optional call disappears entirely — it only ever earned the bonus — so a decisive prediction is just the scoreline, and there’s no “+2” anywhere. (The required penalties pick on a draw is unaffected — it decides who advances regardless of bonus.)

Option A adds the skipped-decider nudge (bonus-on only) + a distinct locked state. Option B ships lighter. My vote is A — the nudge recovers bonus points players would otherwise miss, which is the whole reason the decider exists.
