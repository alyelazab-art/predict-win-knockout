Knockout — “Submitted prediction” state · A vs B

The gap: after submitting a knockout prediction, players could only see the score — never the decider they picked. The fix is the same in both options: after submit you always see what you picked — the score and your decider call (Full time, Extra time, or who wins on Penalties), in the same phone shell as the live editable card. Try both and tell me which you prefer.

The decider bonus is the merchant’s setting, so no amount is shown here. It only affects the nudge for skipping the optional call — it never affects whether your pick is shown. With no bonus, the nudge simply doesn’t appear.

How to read this: both options reuse the real phone shell, header, stats and card — only the body changes after submit. The fix in both: your pick is always visible — the scoreline plus your decider call (Full time / Extra time / Penalties + who advances), as plain labelled text.

Option A adds two things: a nudge when the player skipped the optional Full/Extra-time call (prompts them to add it for the bonus — no amount shown here, since that’s the merchant’s setting; with no bonus the nudge doesn’t appear), and a distinct locked state once the match kicks off. Option B ships lighter — just the pick + Edit.

My vote is A — the nudge recovers bonus points players would otherwise leave on the table, which is the whole reason the decider exists.
